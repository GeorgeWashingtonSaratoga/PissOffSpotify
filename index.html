PissOffSpotify

Hello! Thank YOU for choosing PissOffSpotify over Spotify! We appreciate your support!


As we do not expect money in exchange for this wonderful app, we implore you to recommend this app to your fellow android users!!!


Regarding what kind of audio files you can use, please use only MP3s that have embedded pictures. While using MP3s that do not have embedded pictures still works, it causes extreme lag. We are currently working on a fix for this. Your MP3s do not need any other metadata.


PissOffSpotify supports a variety of languages, being English, Spanish, Italian, French, German, Portuguese (Brazilian), Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Japanese, Korean, Turkish, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Ukrainian, Polish, Persian, Sicilian. Please note that aside from English, Spanish, and Italian, the translations were made using online tools.


Please join our discord!!!! You can report bugs, mistranslations, and suggestions!
